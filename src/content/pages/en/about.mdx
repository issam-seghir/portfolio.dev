## Who am I

I'm a Software Engineer from Algeria, specializing in web and mobile development. I work with React, Next.js, React Native, and Expo on the frontend, and Node.js, Python, and FastAPI on the backend.

My experience spans building ERP systems, AI-powered applications, SaaS platforms, and DevOps workflows. I'm passionate about creating performant, accessible, and well-architected software.

## What I do

- **Frontend**: React, Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI
- **Mobile**: React Native, Expo
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, Python, FastAPI
- **Database**: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Prisma, Drizzle
